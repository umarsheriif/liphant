Liphant - Platform Guide

Your Complete Guide to Finding & Providing Special Needs Therapy Services

Welcome to Liphant

Liphant is Egypt’s leading platform connecting families with qualified shadow teachers and therapy centers. We specialize in supporting children with special needs including ADHD, Autism Spectrum Disorder, Speech Therapy, and more.

What We Offer: - For Parents: Find verified, qualified teachers and therapy centers - For Teachers: Grow your practice and reach families who need you - For Centers: Manage your team, services, and bookings digitally

For Parents

Getting Started

Step 1: Create Your Account

  1. Visit liphant.co/register
  2. Select “I’m a Parent”
  3. Enter your details:
    • Full name
    • Email address
    • Phone number
    • Password
  4. Verify your email
  5. Complete your profile

Step 2: Complete Your Profile

Navigate to: /parent/profile

Add important information: - Your Location: City and district (helps find nearby teachers) - Your Children: Add each child’s name, age, and specific needs - Profile Photo: Helps teachers recognize you

Tip: The more details you provide about your children’s needs, the better we can match you with the right teachers.

Browse Teachers

Search & Filter Options: | Filter | Description | |——–|————-| | Search | Find teachers by name | | City | Filter by location | | Specialization | ADHD, Autism, Speech Therapy, etc. | | Minimum Rating | Show only highly-rated teachers | | Max Hourly Rate | Set your budget limit |

Teacher Profile Information

Each teacher profile shows: - Professional bio (English & Arabic) - Specializations and expertise areas - Years of experience - Education and certifications - Hourly rate - Service area/radius - Weekly availability calendar - Reviews from other parents

Booking a Session

How to Book

  1. Find a Teacher: Browse /teachers and click on a teacher
  2. Check Availability: View their weekly schedule
  3. Click “Book Now”: Select your preferred date and time
  4. Add Notes: Describe your child’s needs or session goals
  5. Confirm: Submit your booking request

Booking Status Guide

Status Meaning
Pending Waiting for teacher to accept
Confirmed Session is scheduled
Completed Session finished successfully
Cancelled Booking was cancelled

Managing Your Bookings

Navigate to: /parent/bookings

Here you can: - View upcoming sessions - See past session history - Cancel bookings if needed - Access session details and notes

After Your Session

Viewing Session Records

Navigate to: /parent/records

Track your child’s progress: - View notes from teachers - Filter by date range - Search by teacher name - Download session documentation

Leaving a Review

After a completed session: 1. Go to your booking 2. Click “Leave Review” 3. Rate your experience (1-5 stars) 4. Add a comment about the session 5. Submit

Your reviews help other parents find great teachers and encourage teachers to maintain high standards.

Finding Therapy Centers

Browse Centers

Navigate to: /centers

Discover verified therapy centers offering: - Multiple specialized services - Professional teams - Structured programs - Modern facilities

Center Services

Each center offers various services like: - Speech Therapy Sessions - Occupational Therapy - ABA Therapy - Social Skills Groups - Parent Training - Early Intervention

Booking Center Services

  1. Visit the center’s profile: /centers/[center-id]
  2. Browse available services
  3. Click “Book This Service”
  4. Select date and available time
  5. A qualified teacher will be assigned by the center

For Teachers

Why Join Liphant?

  • Reach More Families: Access thousands of parents looking for qualified teachers
  • Manage Your Schedule: Set your own availability and rates
  • Build Your Reputation: Collect reviews and grow your profile
  • Get Verified: Stand out with our verification badge
  • Easy Payments: Track earnings and session history

Getting Started

Step 1: Register

  1. Visit liphant.co/register
  2. Select “I’m a Teacher”
  3. Complete registration
  4. Build your professional profile

Step 2: Complete Your Profile

Navigate to: /teacher/profile

Essential Information:

Section What to Include
Bio Your experience, approach, and philosophy (English & Arabic)
Specializations Select all areas you’re qualified in
Experience Years of experience
Education Degrees and institutions
Certifications Professional certifications with dates
Hourly Rate Your session fee
Location City, district, and service radius
Profile Photo Professional headshot

Supported Specializations: - ADHD Support - Autism Spectrum Disorder - Speech & Language Therapy - Occupational Therapy - Behavioral Therapy - Learning Disabilities - Sensory Processing - Developmental Delays - Social Skills Development

Step 3: Set Your Availability

Navigate to: /teacher/availability

Configure your weekly schedule: 1. Select a day (Sunday - Saturday) 2. Add time slots (e.g., 9:00 AM - 12:00 PM) 3. Add multiple slots per day if needed 4. Save your availability

Tip: Keep your availability updated! Parents can only book during your available times.

Step 4: Get Verified

After completing your profile: 1. Our team reviews your credentials 2. We verify your education and certifications 3. Upon approval, you receive a Verified Badge 4. Start receiving booking requests!

Managing Bookings

Receiving Booking Requests

Navigate to: /teacher/bookings

When a parent requests a session: 1. You’ll receive a notification 2. View the booking details: - Parent information - Child’s needs (from notes) - Requested date/time - Session amount 3. Accept or Decline the request

Session Workflow

Booking Request → Accept → Confirmed Session → Complete Session → Get Paid

After Each Session

  1. Mark the session as Completed
  2. Add session notes (optional but recommended)
  3. Review appears on your profile
  4. Earnings are tracked in your dashboard

Your Dashboard

Navigate to: /teacher/dashboard

Track your performance: - Today’s Sessions: Upcoming bookings - Total Earnings: Monthly and all-time - Rating: Your average rating - Reviews: What parents say about you - Messages: Unread conversations

Session Records

Navigate to: /teacher/records

Document your work: - Add notes after each session - Track progress across sessions - Filter by parent or date - Build comprehensive case histories

Best Practices for Teachers

Profile Tips

  • Use a professional, friendly photo
  • Write a warm, informative bio
  • List all relevant certifications
  • Keep your availability current
  • Respond to messages promptly

Session Tips

  • Confirm sessions 24 hours before
  • Arrive on time
  • Document progress notes
  • Communicate with parents
  • Ask for reviews after good sessions

Managing Your Services

Navigate to: /center/services

Creating a Service

  1. Click “Add Service”
  2. Fill in service details:
    • Service name (English & Arabic)
    • Description
    • Price per session
    • Duration (minutes)
  3. Save and publish

Assigning Teachers to Services

Navigate to: /center/services/[service-id]

  1. Open service details
  2. Go to “Teacher Assignments”
  3. Select which teachers can provide this service
  4. Only assigned teachers appear as available for booking

Managing Your Team

Navigate to: /center/teachers

Adding Teachers

  1. Teachers must first create their own Liphant account
  2. Invite them to join your center
  3. Assign roles (Staff, Manager, Lead)
  4. Set employment type (Full-time, Part-time, Contract)

Team Dashboard

View at a glance: - All center teachers - Their specializations - Availability status - Performance metrics

Handling Bookings

Navigate to: /center/bookings

Booking Types

Direct Teacher Bookings: - Parents book specific teachers at your center - Teacher handles confirmation

Center Service Bookings: - Parents book a service (not specific teacher) - You assign an available teacher - Status: “Awaiting Assignment” until teacher assigned

Booking Management

Tab Shows
Awaiting Assignment Bookings needing teacher assignment
Pending Awaiting confirmation
Confirmed Scheduled sessions
Completed Finished sessions

Assigning Teachers to Bookings

  1. Go to “Awaiting Assignment” tab
  2. Select a booking
  3. Choose an available teacher
  4. Click “Assign”
  5. Booking status changes to “Confirmed”
