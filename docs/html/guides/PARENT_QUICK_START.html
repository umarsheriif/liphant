Liphant - Parent Quick Start Guide

Find the Right Support for Your Child

Welcome to Liphant!

We connect families with verified shadow teachers and therapy centers specialized in supporting children with special needs.

What You Can Find

  • Shadow Teachers - One-on-one support specialists
  • Therapy Centers - Professional therapy services
  • Specializations - ADHD, Autism, Speech Therapy, and more
  • Community - Connect with other parents

Getting Started (5 Minutes)

1. Create Your Account

Go to: liphant.co/register - Select “I’m a Parent” - Enter your details - Verify your email

2. Complete Your Profile

Go to: liphant.co/parent/profile

Add important info: - Your location (city & district) - Your children’s details (name, age, needs) - Profile photo

Tip: The more details you share about your child’s needs, the better we can match you with the right teachers.

Finding Teachers

Browse Teachers

Go to: liphant.co/teachers

Filter by: | Option | Find Teachers Who… | |——–|———————| | City | Are near you | | Specialization | Match your child’s needs | | Rating | Have great reviews | | Hourly Rate | Fit your budget |

What You’ll See

Each teacher profile shows: - Bio and experience - Specializations - Certifications - Hourly rate - Availability calendar - Reviews from other parents

Booking a Session

  1. Find a Teacher - Browse and select one
  2. Check Availability - View their calendar
  3. Click “Book Now” - Select date & time
  4. Add Notes - Describe your child’s needs
  5. Confirm - Submit booking request

Booking Status

Status Meaning
Pending Waiting for teacher to accept
Confirmed Session is scheduled
Completed Session finished
Cancelled Booking cancelled

Track bookings at: liphant.co/parent/bookings

Finding Therapy Centers

Browse Centers

Go to: liphant.co/centers

Find professional therapy centers offering: - Multiple services - Qualified teams - Structured programs

Booking Center Services

  1. Visit a center’s profile
  2. Browse their services
  3. Click “Book This Service”
  4. Select date and time
  5. The center assigns a qualified teacher

After Your Session

Session Records

Leave a Review

Help other parents by reviewing your experience: 1. Go to completed booking 2. Click “Leave Review” 3. Rate (1-5 stars) and comment 4. Submit
